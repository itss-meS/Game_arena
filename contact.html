Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How do I submit a game tip?

Use the contact form above, select "Tips Submission" as the subject, and describe your tip in detail. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and features the best ones.

Can I advertise on GameArena?

Yes! We offer banner placements, sponsored articles, and game feature packages. Choose "Partnership / Collaboration" in the contact form for our media kit and rates.

Do you cover mobile and PC games?

Absolutely. GameArena covers games across all platforms — Android, iOS, PC, PlayStation, and Xbox. We aim to represent the full spectrum of the global gaming community.

How can I join your Discord community?

Click the Discord link in the footer or visit discord.gg/gamearena. Our server has channels for each featured game, general chat, tournament announcements, and a tips-sharing hub.
